The Challenge

Poverty managed,
not permanently reduced.

In Rwanda, most development initiatives focus on short-term aid and basic skills training — leaving vulnerable people in cycles of dependency. Kimuntu Rising breaks that cycle permanently.

  • 🔗
    Continued Dependency

    Beneficiaries rely on external support with no path to independence

  • 📉
    No Market-Linked Training

    Skills programs disconnected from actual job market demands

  • 🧠
    Unaddressed Psychological Barriers

    Trauma, low confidence, and lack of purpose remain untreated

  • 🚶
    Zero Post-Training Follow-up

    Participants left without mentorship after initial training ends

"Poverty is often managed rather than permanently reduced — we change that."

Core Programs

Four Pillars of Change

Each program builds on the last — creating a complete journey from inner transformation to community impact.

01

Rise Mind Program

Focus: Personal Transformation

A deep-dive into the inner work. Participants undergo mindset and behavioral change coaching, trauma-informed support, and leadership development to rebuild identity and purpose.

Mindset Coaching Trauma Healing Leadership
02

Skills-to-Income Program

Focus: Employability & Income Creation

Market-driven vocational and digital skills training paired with real apprenticeships and practical income-generating projects linked to actual job market demand.

Vocational Skills Digital Training Apprenticeships
03

Micro-Enterprise Incubation

Focus: Business Creation & Sustainability

From idea to operating business. Participants receive startup support, business training, access to seed capital through group-based savings, and continuous mentorship.

Startup Support Seed Capital Mentorship
04

Community Impact Network

Focus: Long-Term Sustainability

Alumni become mentors. A peer support and opportunity-sharing ecosystem that sustains transformation beyond the program — creating lasting community-level change.

Peer Support Alumni Network Opportunity Hub

Launch Phase

The Pilot Program

A focused, fundable proof-of-concept designed to demonstrate the Kimuntu Rising model at scale.

Program Overview

10 Beneficiaries
6 Months

A tight, measurable pilot that proves the transformation model works — generating the evidence base needed for full-scale funding and replication.

Phase 1: Beneficiary selection and onboarding
Month 1–2: Mindset transformation (Rise Mind Program)
Month 2–4: Skills training + apprenticeship placement
Month 4–6: Business incubation and income generation
Ongoing: Mentorship, follow-up and performance tracking
